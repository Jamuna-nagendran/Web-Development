"The Solitary Reaper" is a lyric by English Romantic poet William Wordsworth, and one of his best-known works, The poem was inspired by him and his sister Doraty's stay at the village of Strathyre in the parish of Balquhidder in scotland in spetember 1803. "The Solitary Reaper" is one of Wordsorth's most famous post- Lyrical Ballads lyrics. The Wordsorthof the reaper's song are incomprehensible to the speaker, so his attention os free to focus on the tone, expressive beauty and the blissful mood it creates in him. The poem function to "praise the beauty of music and its fluid expressive beauty", the "spontaneous overflow of powerful feelings: it takes its origin from emotion recollected in traquillity" that Wordsorth identified at the heart of poetry.
